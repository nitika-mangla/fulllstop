Whirl the World - Bookings

All bookings for travel must be made via the Internal Bookings Department of Whirl the World.

Train / Bus / Coach Travel

Please book via the company intranet at least seven days in advance.

Where this is not possible, telephone or email using the details shown on the company intranet.

Air Travel

Please book via the company intranet at least one month in advance.

Where this is not possible, telephone or email using the details shown on the company intranet.
